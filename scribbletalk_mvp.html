💡 Offline reinforcement tips
🖊️
Give large crayons and big paper. Encourage scribbling without correction — every mark is a word.
🔊
Say the letter sound while your child traces it. "Ssss like snake" is more memorable than the letter name alone.
📸
Photograph your child's scribbles and ask "tell me about this." Their explanation is expressive language — celebrate it.
🌿
Short sessions (5–8 minutes) are more effective than long ones. Stop before your child wants to — leave them wanting more.
